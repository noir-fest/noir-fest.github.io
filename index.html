NOIR FEST

\ ˈnwär ˌfest \

(noun)

a traveling film festival increasing access to visionary, world-class Black Diaspora film and media for theater audiences around the world.

Help us put Black dreams on screen

The NOIR FEST team believes that Blackness equates to cultural richness, persistent innovation, eternal beauty and global significance. We believe that Black futures are bright worldwide, and we seek to bring a multisensory experience of that future to those who seek it.

Problem

Blacks in global media plagued by: Lack of representation, Stereotypes & tropes, #OscarsSoWhite

Independent Black Diaspora Filmmakers limited by Film distribution channels, Gatekeepers of Hollywood

Solution: Noirfest

Highly curated global Black content Paired with the African Diaspora: New York, DC, Los Angeles, Chicago
